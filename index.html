Mobile Suit Gundam

The ever growing franchise of the Mobile Suit Gundam or MSG in short has multiple series, characters, Mobile Suits, and Gundams. Here are some of my favorites.

ASW-G-08 Gundam Barbatos Lupus

The Gundam Barbatos Lupus comes from the Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron Blooded Orphans series. This Gundam is piloted by the beloved MC, Mikazuki Augus. This Gundam makes an appearance during the first episode of the second season being a development after Gundam Barbatos. The devil of Tekkadan, Barbatos.


X-EX01 Gundam Calibarn

The Gundam Calibarn comes from the Mobile Suit Gundam: Witch From Mercury series. This Gundam is piloted by the MC, Suletta Mercury. This Gundam only makes an appearance in the last episode of the series. The Witch from Mercury, Calibarn


GN-0000+GNR-010 00 Raiser

The 00 Raiser comes from the Mobile Suit Gundam: 00 series. This Gundam, if you can even call it that, is piloted by the MC Setsuna F. Seiei. This "Gundam" first made an appearance during the second season of Gundam 00. The angel, 00 Raiser
